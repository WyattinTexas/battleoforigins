# BOO Multiplayer — The Simple Version

## The Screen
Almost nothing. You see:

1. **Your cards** — the ones you own, that's it
2. **Your packs** — unopened packs waiting for you  
3. **The leaderboard** — who's on top
4. **Queue up** — one button, find an opponent
5. **Games remaining today** — you get X games per day
6. **Badges** — how many you need, what you've earned

Nothing else. No settings menu, no deck builder screen, no stats page. Cards. Packs. Leaderboard. Play. Done.

## The Feel
- Open the page → see your cards laid out beautifully
- A glowing pack if you have one to open → tap it, cards flip one by one
- Big "BATTLE" button → queue up, matched in seconds
- Pick your 3 from your owned cards → fight
- Win → badge progress, leaderboard climbs
- Lose → learn, try again tomorrow if you're out of games
- That's the whole loop

## Energy System
- X games per day (3? 5?)
- Resets daily
- Encourages you to make each game COUNT
- "I shouldn't have lost that one" feeling — because you only had 3 chances

## Badges
- Daily/weekly goals
- "Win 2 games today" → badge
- "Open 5 packs" → badge  
- "Reach top 10 on leaderboard" → badge
- Visual, collectible, something to chase

## What We're NOT Building
- No chat
- No friends list
- No deck saving
- No tournament mode
- No spectating
- None of that. Just play.

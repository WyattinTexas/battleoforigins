How to Play
A Spirit's Guide to Battle
1 The Basics
Pick 3 Spiritkin for your team. Your lead ghost fights first.
🎲
Both players roll dice each round. Highest roll wins and deals damage.
KO a ghost? The sideline sends in the next fighter at full HP.
🏆
KO all 3 enemy ghosts to win the battle!

4 Abilities
Every Spiritkin has a unique ability that triggers automatically during battle — on entry, on a win, from the sideline, or other conditions. Read the ability text on each card to plan your strategy. Some ghosts work best as leads, others shine from the bench.
5 Items
Some Spiritkin create items — persistent power-ups that stay active for your whole team, even if the creator is KO'd. Items can boost damage, add burn, grant extra dice, and more.
For example, Finn crafts the Flame Blade — giving your team +1 die and +3 Burn on wins for the rest of the battle.

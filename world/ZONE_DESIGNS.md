# Zone Design Bible — Battle of Origins

Each zone is a full 110x85 tile map. Players transition between zones at map edges.

---

## 🏔️ FROST VALLEY
**Theme:** Vast frozen wilderness, mystery, discovery, exploration

### Areas:
- **Polaris** (main town) — Cozy hub with inn, shops, arena, warm lantern lights against snow
- **Frosty Woods** — Dense snowy forest, winding paths, hidden clearings, wildlife sprites
- **Frozen Lake District** — Sprawling frozen lakes, running rivers, ice fishing spots, frozen waterfalls
- **The Mountain** — Towering peaks, Mountain King's lair at summit, cave networks, cliffside paths
- **Ice Dungeon** — Crystalline dungeon entrance carved into a glacier, glowing blue corridors
- **Logging Camp** — Second smaller settlement deep in the woods, rugged, functional
- **Aurora Fields** — Open tundra with northern lights feel, rare encounters, peaceful
- **The Frozen Waterfall** — Massive landmark visible from afar, partially climbable
- **Crystal Caverns** — Underground ice caves that glow blue, connects mountain to dungeon
- **Hidden Mystery** — An unmarked discovery location, rewards pure exploration

### Terrain: snow, ice, frost trees, frozen water, mountains, paths, buildings
### Color Palette: whites, ice blues, deep navy, warm amber (buildings)

## 🌿 ROLLING HILLS
**Theme:** Stardew Valley meets the Shire — warm, alive, pastoral, layered

### Areas:
- **Meadowbrook** (main town) — Charming farm town, market square, herbalist, tavern
- **The Meadows** — Wide open flower fields, butterflies, rolling green hills, panoramic views
- **Timber's Woods** — Deep deciduous forest where Timber lives, thick canopy, dappled light, mushrooms. Deep interior gets darker/denser — reward for exploration
- **The Swamp Farm** — Misty wetland with rickety bridges, rice paddies, unique crops, frogs
- **Windmill Ridge** — High ground with windmills, overlook of the whole zone, breezy
- **River Valley** — Winding river cutting through the zone, fishing spots, stone bridges
- **The Old Mill** — Abandoned structure with history, quest location
- **Festival Grounds** — Area that feels like it hosts events, gives life to the zone

### Terrain: grass, flowers, warm trees, rolling hills, water, farmland, paths
### Color Palette: greens, warm yellows, earth browns, wildflower colors

## 🌋 VOLCANIC ISLES
**Theme:** Tropical paradise surrounding deadly volcanic core — water & fire

### Areas:
- **The Beaches** — Cool sandy shores, palm trees, tide pools, safe starting area
- **Open Ocean** — Water surrounds the central volcano. Crossing methods: stepping stones, raft paths, tidal bridges
- **The Volcano** — Massive central landmark, lava rivers flowing outward, obsidian formations, magma crystals
- **Volcano Interior** — Cross the water to enter. Lava caves, the Forge, rare encounters
- **Blue Lagoons** — Hidden tropical pools, waterfalls, peaceful exploration
- **Volcanic Settlement** — Trader outpost on coast: inn, forge, arena (The Crucible)
- **Obsidian Shores** — Black sand beach where lava meets ocean, steam vents everywhere
- **Tidal Crossing** — The path across the water, appears/disappears with tides

### Terrain: sand, water, lava, obsidian, ash, palm trees, volcanic rock
### Color Palette: ocean blues, sandy gold, lava orange/red, obsidian black, tropical green

## 🏰 DARK CASTLE
**Theme:** An entire dark city/village — massive, imposing, lived-in darkness

### Areas:
- **The Village Streets** — Winding cobblestone streets, dark market stalls, lantern-lit alleys. Takes up MOST of the map
- **The Grand Castle** — Enormous centerpiece structure, multiple wings, throne room
- **Shadow Market** — Bustling underground trade hub, rare items, shady NPCs
- **The Undercrypt** — Dungeon network beneath the castle
- **Demon Quarter** — Residential area with dark houses, eerie gardens, NPC homes
- **The Antechamber** — Castle entrance hall, sets the tone
- **Outer Walls & Gates** — Fortified perimeter, guard towers, main gate
- **The Clocktower** — Tallest point, view the whole village from up there
- **Verticality** — Towers, rooftops, underground passages. Not just flat streets

### Terrain: dark stone, cobblestone paths, dark walls, castle floors, dead trees
### Color Palette: deep purples, charcoal, blood red accents, ghostly greens, torch amber
